Forecast Simulator

Market Intelligence

The system scans the competitive landscape, extracts structured intelligence from unstructured news, scores each finding for reliability, and feeds it into the forecast.

1

Scan

Automated search across 40+ trusted sources for competitor activity, regulatory changes, and industry trends across 16 HVAC competitors.

2

Extract

A language model pulls out event type, company, products affected, impact direction, and a supporting quote from each article.

3

Score

Every event gets a trust score from four factors: source reputation, extraction confidence, corroboration, and recency.

4

Quantify

Trusted events are converted into projected market share impact using stated figures, historical analogues, or reference benchmarks.

Event Types Tracked

  • Product launches
  • Pricing changes
  • Supply disruptions
  • Regulatory changes
  • Capacity expansions
  • Market entries and exits

Trust Tiers

  • Official Source (85% +)
  • Verified Source (70% +)
  • News Source (55% +)
  • Unverified (40% +)
  • Low Confidence (<40%)
