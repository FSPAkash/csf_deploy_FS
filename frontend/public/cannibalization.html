Scenario Simulator

Cannibalization Modeling

When one Manufacturer product grows, it can take volume from another. This model measures that transfer using historical data, then lets you simulate scenarios by setting parameters manually or letting the system derive them.

1

Load

Monthly sales forecast for both the source and target product. A transfer ratio is computed for each month.

2

Analyze

Market share trends, seasonal patterns, and periods where the two products moved in opposite directions.

3

Configure

Manual mode with user-set parameters, or intelligent mode where the system picks the best method from the data.

4

Simulate

A 4-equation system runs month by month: source loss, target gain, seasonal adjustment, and ramp curve.

5

Assess

A data quality score (0-7) across seven factors determines the width of confidence bands.

Product Families

  • FN - Furnaces (80 Multi, 80 Var)
  • HP - Heat Pumps (1PH)
  • For beta testing

Key Concepts

  • Transfer ratio = target / (source + target)
  • Ramp curves: linear or logistic (S-curve)
  • Seasonal modulation from historical indices
  • Confidence bands scale with data quality
